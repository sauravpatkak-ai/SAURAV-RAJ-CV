About Me

I am Saurav Raj, a postgraduate researcher in Genetics and Plant Breeding currently pursuing my M.Sc. at Rajmata Vijayaraje Scindia Krishi Vishwavidyalaya (ICAR), Gwalior.

My research focuses on plant genetic diversity, yield improvement, and quantitative genetics approaches for crop improvement. I have experience in field experimentation, statistical analysis, and molecular laboratory techniques.

Alongside research, I actively contribute to scientific publishing, academic editorial platforms, and digital knowledge dissemination in agricultural sciences.
